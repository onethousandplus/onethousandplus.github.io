Will you be counted?


1000plus Day of Service is CMU's largest day of service, and occurs every semester! Our aim is to get over 1000 volunteers from the CMU Community to help out all over the greater Pittsburgh area doing community service! It is a great opportunity to join our campus community in traveling throughout the Pittsburgh area and making a difference while having fun and meeting new people. If you are interested in participating, please sign up using the link below. It just asks for your name, contact info, and if there is an organization you are a member of that you would like to volunteer with. You can select "individual" if you are not affiliated with any of the groups.
Registration will close on September 27th!
