2016 Spring Volunteer Sites



+ 52Lots: African Healing Garden

Volunteer Activities: Building a dry stack wall, moving dirt with shovels and wheel barrels, helping to create a small natural pond, and basic site cleanup.


+ Animal Friends

Volunteer Activities: Activities may vary but might include; landscaping (weather dependent), making enrichment items for cats and rabbits, shelter cleaning, donation sorting. Day includes a tour of the facility and a supervised animal interaction.


+ Animal Rescue League Wildlife Center

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor trail maintenance.


+ Arsenal Family & Children's Center

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor beautification. Possible outdoor painting.


+ Bloomfield Garfield Corporation

Volunteer Activities: Building beautification.


+ Camp Guyasuta

Volunteer Activities: Clearing trails, cutting brush, chipping brush, splitting logs with log splitter, removing pool cover, landscaping, litter pickup, stream bank work, painting.


+ Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh (Main, Children's Department)

Volunteer Activities: Cleaning books, checking to be sure all labels are in place, pulling any that are damaged, being sure books are in order.


+ Children's School

Volunteer Activities: Spring garden clean-up, planting of new plants, outdoor beautification.


+ Craig Street Project

Volunteer Activities: Trash pick-up, sweeping sidewalks, window washing, planting (depends on weather).


+ Fairmont Apartments

Volunteer Activities: Resident socialization (Bingo, Games, Crafts, etc).


+ First Trinty: Monthly Mercies

Volunteer Activities:

9:30-11:30: Laying out a goodwill store, and laying out clothing and other toiletries for people to come take.

12:30-3:30: Clients arrive and volunteers will assist in getting clients to the sign-ups, and finding clothing for the clients, and sizes, and also clean-up afterward.


+ Friends of the Riverfront

Volunteer Activities: Working on tree care maintenance, planting flower bulbs, picking up litter, and mitigation of invasive species.


+ Garfield Community Action Team (Kincaid Street Community Garden)

Volunteer Activities: Preparing a community garden for the upcoming gardening season (spreading mulch, repairing a fence, helping kids to paint bird houses, spreading compost).


+ Global Links

Volunteer Activities: Sorting and packing medical supplies to help improve healthcare in resource-poor communities.


+ Grass Roots Green Homes

Volunteer Activities: Canvassing the Oakland neighborhood, particularly South Oakland, to inform and recruit residents to participate in Grass Roots Green Homes, a program to educate and provide people in the Oakland neighborhood with energy efficiency resources.


+ Hosanna House

Volunteer Activities: Gardening, beautifying, and cleaning outdoors on 14 acres,


+ Just Harvest

Volunteer Activities: Assembling a mailing for the Spring membership drive.


+ Light of Life Rescue Mission

Volunteer Activities: Packing bagged lunches off site and delivering them to the shelter.


+ MADDADS

Volunteer Activities: A think tank for a social media strategy for MADDADS to gain visibility.


+ Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor beautification. Projects will include removing graffiti, painting, litter/trash removal, cleaning public fixtures, etc.


+ Pittsburgh Parks Conservancy

Volunteer Activities: Working in the Frick Woods removing invasive species and picking up litter.


+ Shadyside Chamber of Commerce

Volunteer Activities: Clean up litter on Walnut Street, Ellsworth Avenue, South Highland Avenue and outside streets.


+ South Side Slopes Neighborhood Association

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor beautification, trash pick up, dump site clean up. Will be working on a number of public stairways and moving around a walking route within the neighborhood of the South Side Slopes.


+ The Corner

Volunteer Activities: Engaging West Oakland residents in quality of life and priority surveys for the EcoInnovation district planning process.


+ The First Tee of Pittsburgh

Volunteer Activities: Indoor and outdoor beautification.


+ The Salvation Army Homewood Brushton Worship and Service Center

Volunteer Activities: Cleaning, decorating, and organizing of inside of building. Includes shampooing of carpets.


+ Trail Pittsburgh

Volunteer Activities: Will be hiking the trails and removing garbage along the trails and in the woods. The garbage will be collected in bags and hauled to trail heads where the garbage will be collected by the park to be removed from the park staff.


+ Tree Pittsburgh

Volunteer Activities: Plant propagation, up-potting, helping in the nursery.


+ Veterans Place of Washington Boulevard

Volunteer Activities: Organization and cleaning.


+ Vintage

Volunteer Activities: Will be helping to set up for a Flea Market. Includes sorting and pricing items, setting up table displays and signage.


+ Western PA Humane Society

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor litter pick up, building enrichment toys, cleaning in the shelter, laundry, dishes.


+ Wilkinsburg Borough Council

Volunteer Activities: Gardening and outdoor beautification.
