About 1000plus Day of Service

1000plus Day of Service is a Carnegie Mellon day of service, where students, faculty, staff, and alumni have an opportunity to positively impact the greater Pittsburgh community by engaging in service efforts. This year, the Fall Day of Service will take place on Saturday, October 4th, 2014. The 1000plus planning committee has worked to provide multiple volunteer sites throughout the greater Pittsburgh area. It is our goal to mobilize over 1000 members of Carnegie Mellon community to serve all over Pittsburgh, allowing them to have a personally relevant impact in the local area.

Spring 2014 Day of Service Stats:

  1. 904 volunteers
  2. 46 large group participants
  3. 26 sites
  4. 21 buses

We also hope that 1000plus is a chance to connect and network with people of different interests that share the common goal of promoting service!
