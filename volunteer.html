2015 Fall Volunteer Sites



+ Arsenal Family & Children's Center

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor beautification (i.e. Raking leaves, weeding, sweeping, etc.)


+ Beechview Community Garden

Volunteer Activities: Prepping the garden for winter through weeding, pulling out annuals, turning compost, mowing, laying landscape fabric, mulching, etc.


+ Camp Guyasuta

Volunteer Activities: Splitting logs with gas splitter, landscaping, clearing invasive plant species, stream bank restoration, winterize kayaks & canoes, tune up mt. bikes, gutter repaires, miscellaneous camp maintanence projects.


+ Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh

Volunteer Activities: Volunteers will be helping in the Children's Department at the Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh. Volunteers will assist with cleaning, shelving, and shifting books.


+ Children's School

Volunteer Activities: Gardening (specifically spreading topsoil and mulch)


+ Craig Street Infrastucture Committee

Volunteer Activities: Trash pick-up, sweep sidewalks, planting, mulching, washing windows.


+ Garfield Community Action Team

Volunteer Activities: Gardening and tree tending.


+ Global Links

Volunteer Activities: Pack medical supplies.


+ Grow Pittsburgh

Volunteer Activities: Harvesting food, mulching, and assisting with greenhouse duties.


+ Hazelwood Initiative/PCRG

Volunteer Activities: Gardening, outdoor beautification, and litter clean-up.


+ Hosanna House, Inc

Volunteer Activities: Clean tables and chairs and put them away for storage, clean up front and back of Pavillion, clear debris and dead trees.


+ In Service of Seniors

Volunteer Activities: Yard clean-up and raking leaves for seniors age 60+ in the greater Pittsburgh area.


+ Just Harvest

Volunteer Activities: Sorting and photographing silent auction donations, and making calls to invited guests.


+ Light of Life Rescue Mission

Volunteer Activities: Create 150 bagged lunches on CMU's campus and bring them down to Light of Life.


+ Oakland Planning and Development Corporation

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor beautification in the Oakland Square Historic District.


+ Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership

Volunteer Activities: Leaf removal, graffiti removal, litter/trash clean-up, possibly painting, etc.


+ Shadyside Boys & Girls Club

Volunteer Activities: Assisting with Run Shadyside. Volunteers will help closing streets as well as cleaning up following post-race party.


+ Shadyside Chamber of Commerce

Volunteer Activities: Outdoor beautification through litter clean-up.


+ The Salvation Army

Volunteer Activities: Gardening and outdoor beautification.


+ Tree Pittsburgh

Volunteer Activities: Volunteers will be helping to clear invasive vines from a future tree planting site in West Penn Park.


+ Ward Home, Inc.

Volunteer Activities: The project will consist of light construction/demolition, painting, and cleaning at the teen boy's location.


+ Western PA Humane Society

Volunteer Activities: Beautification and enrichment making.
